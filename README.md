# Zombie-Rabbit-game 🥕

**Zombie Rabbit** — Collect 10 zombies before they eat your crops!

## The Story
Your precious veggie patch is under attack! A bunch of fluffy bunnies got infected and turned into carrot-craving zombies. They are popping out of their burrows, ready to feast on your harvest.

You need to act fast and catch **10 zombie rabbits** to save your garden. But watch your fingers! Not all rabbits are infected. Some are just innocent, healthy bunnies hiding from the chaos. If you accidentally whack a normal rabbit... well, let's just say you'll feel terrible, and it's an instant **GAME OVER**.

## How to Play
- **The Target:** Keep a sharp eye on the burrows. When a half-zombie rabbit pops up, **CLICK IT** to catch it and earn a star!
- **The Goal:** Collect exactly **10 stars** to clear the garden and win the game.
- **The Danger:** DO NOT touch the normal rabbits! Hitting a healthy bunny instantly ends your run.
